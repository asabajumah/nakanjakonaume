About Nakanjako Naume

Nakanjako Naume is a Ugandan author. She is best known for being the wife of famous Ugandan actor, musician, and author Asaba Jumah.

Born on 23 December 2005 in Hoima, Uganda, she began her writing career in 2024 and has continued to contribute to literature through creative storytelling and publishing.

Since 11 April 2025, Nakanjako Naume has been married to Asaba Jumah. Together they have participated in literary and creative projects.
